Osa "following-sibling" obsahuje všechny následující sourozence uzlu, který je v kontextu.
 
/AAA/BBB/following-sibling::*

     <AAA>
          <BBB>
               <CCC/>
               <DDD/>
          </BBB>
          <XXX>
               <DDD>
                    <EEE/>
                    <DDD/>
                    <CCC/>
                    <FFF/>
                    <FFF>
                         <GGG/>
                    </FFF>
               </DDD>
          </XXX>
          <CCC>
               <DDD/>
          </CCC>
     </AAA>
